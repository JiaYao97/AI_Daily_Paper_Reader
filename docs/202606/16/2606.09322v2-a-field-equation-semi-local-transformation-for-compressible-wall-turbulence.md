## 摘要
可压缩性和壁面传热通过平均密度和黏性场改变壁湍流的内尺度。大多数半局部变换是在选定壁法向剖面后应用的。这里，变换坐标和变换速度在提取任何剖面之前由壁面锚定的场方程定义。壁面密度和摩擦速度作为辅助场方程的边界数据输入；由此产生的参考密度和摩擦速度，连同局部密度和黏性，设定了壁面层的黏性尺度。引入局部对数密度对比作为坐标拉伸因子的有界修正。对于弱密度变化，修正后的拉伸遵循局部逆运动黏性变化，而有界形式限制了有限密度对比的影响。变换坐标 $Y^+$ 和变换速度 $\bm{U}^+$ 通过使用修正的拉伸和平均黏性剪切从场方程获得。在常物性极限下，密度对比消失，$Y^+$ 退化为普通壁面坐标，每个变换速度分量退化为壁面单位下的常规平均速度分量。对于槽道流，使用壁面边界数据求解场方程，提取的剖面保留其自身的壁面原点。一个冷却激波/边界层相互作用考察了当壁面密度和摩擦速度沿流向变化时的响应。在本文考虑的冷却高速边界层中，有界密度修正缩小了内层和缓冲层的剖面散布。

## Abstract
Compressibility and wall heat transfer change the inner scaling of wall turbulence through the mean density and viscosity fields. Most semi-local transformations are applied after a wall-normal profile has been selected. Here the transformed coordinate and transformed velocity are defined by wall-anchored field equations before any profile is extracted. Wall density and friction velocity enter as boundary data for auxiliary field equations; the resulting reference density and friction velocity, together with the local density and viscosity, set the viscous scaling in the wall layer. The local logarithmic density contrast is introduced as a bounded correction to the coordinate-stretching factor. For weak density variation the corrected stretch follows the local inverse kinematic-viscosity variation, while the bounded form limits the influence of finite density contrasts. The transformed coordinate $Y^+$ and transformed velocity $\bm{U}^+$ are obtained from field equations using the corrected stretching and the mean viscous shear. In the constant-property limit the density contrast vanishes, $Y^+$ reduces to the ordinary wall coordinate and each transformed velocity component reduces to the conventional mean velocity component in wall units. For channel flows, the field equations are solved with wall boundary data and the extracted profiles retain their own wall origins. A cooled shock/boundary-layer interaction examines the response when the wall density and friction velocity vary in the streamwise direction.
