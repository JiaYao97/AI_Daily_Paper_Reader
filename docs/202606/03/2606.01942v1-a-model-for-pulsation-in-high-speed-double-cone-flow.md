## 摘要
在Mach 6条件下，对经典双锥体上的周期性大尺度激波非定常性（文献中称为“脉动”）进行了实验研究。一般双锥体几何由三个无量纲几何参数定义：前锥角和后锥角（θ₁和θ₂），以及锥体斜长比（Λ）。虽然现有关于脉动的文献提供了详细的定性和现象学讨论，但缺乏通过解析方法理解非定常流动现象的途径。本研究旨在填补这一空白。针对θ₁ = 0°和θ₂ = 90°的特定双锥体构型（通常称为尖头-圆柱），在Λ参数空间中研究了自持流动脉动。通过高速纹影成像和时间分辨压力测量，在非定常流场中进行了实验。观察到无量纲脉动频率（斯特劳哈尔数）随Λ单调增加。纹影和压力数据表明，非定常性由循环过程驱动，该过程涉及后锥体附近高压气体的形成及其随后通过前锥体上形成的分离区域膨胀。基于此理解，建立了一个详细的流动解析模型，无需经验参数。该模型成功预测了实验测量的斯特劳哈尔数，并深入理解了驱动流动脉动的机制。

## Abstract
Periodic large-scale shock-wave unsteadiness over a canonical double cone, termed in literature as "pulsation," is experimentally studied at Mach 6. The general double cone geometry is defined by three non-dimensional geometric parameters: fore- and aft-cone angles ($θ_1$ and $θ_2$), and ratio of the conical slant lengths ($\mathitΛ$). While existing literature on pulsation offers detailed qualitative and phenomenological discussions, it is seen that analytical approaches to obtain insight into the unsteady flow phenomena are missing. The present effort is aimed at addressing this gap. Self-sustained flow pulsations for a particular double cone configuration with $θ_1 = 0^\circ$ and $θ_2 = 90^\circ$, commonly referred to as the spike-cylinder, is investigated in the $\mathitΛ$ parameter space. High-speed schlieren imaging and time-resolved pressure measurements are performed in the unsteady flow. The non-dimensional pulsation frequency (Strouhal number) is observed to increase monotonically with $\mathitΛ$. Schlieren and pressure data suggest that the unsteadiness is driven by a cyclic process involving the formation of high-pressure gas near the aft-cone and its subsequent expansion through the separation region formed over the fore-cone. Building on this understanding, a detailed analytical model for the flow is developed with no empirical parameters. The model successfully predicts the experimentally-measured Strouhal number, and provides an in-depth understanding of the mechanisms that drive flow pulsations.
