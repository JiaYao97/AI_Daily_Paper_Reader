## 摘要
预测管道流中湍流的转捩仍是流体力学中的一个基本问题。我们采用变分方法计算雷诺数Re≤5000时层流的最优非线性扰动。随着Re增加，最优扰动在结构上保持相似，但越来越局域化，其厚度标度为δ_r ∝ Re^{-1/3}。它们通过Orr机制增长，随后经历斜波强非线性相互作用阶段和抬升阶段。Orr阶段的能量增益随Re线性增加，且与初始扰动能量E_0无关。斜波和抬升阶段的能量增益受非线性支配，标度为∝ Re^2。我们发现，无论雷诺数大小，只要扰动能量超过恒定阈值，就会发生转捩。因此，在管道流中引发转捩所需的最小扰动能量标度为O(Re^{-3})。

## Abstract
Predicting the transition of turbulence in pipe flow remains a fundamental problem in fluid dynamics. We use a variational approach to compute nonlinear optimal perturbations to the laminar flow at Reynolds number $Re\leq 5000$. As $Re$ increases, optimal perturbations remain structurally similar, but increasingly localize while their thickness scales as $δ_r \propto Re^{-1/3}$. They grow via the Orr mechanism, followed by a phase of strong nonlinear interaction of oblique waves and a lift-up phase. The energy gain during the Orr phase increases linearly with $Re$ and is independent of the initial perturbation energy, $E_0$. The energy gain during the oblique and lift-up phases is governed by nonlinearities and scales as $\propto Re^2$. We find that regardless of the Reynolds number, transition occurs if the energy of the perturbation exceeds a constant threshold. As a result, the minimum perturbation energy required to cause transition in pipe flow scales as $\mathcal{O}(Re^{-3})$.
