## 摘要
在高能重离子碰撞中，高能部分子穿过夸克-胶子等离子体（QGP）时沉积到介质中的能量传播会导致马赫锥状的喷注诱导介质响应。对这种喷注诱导介质响应的完整模拟需要完整的模型，例如耦合的线性玻尔兹曼输运与流体力学（CoLBT-hydro）模型，该模型能够同时处理硬部分子和介质的演化。然而，这种在并行计算机上的完整模拟非常耗费资源，替代的模拟方法将有助于更广泛的物理研究。在本研究中，我们利用CoLBT-hydro模型在√s_{NN} = 5.02 TeV的0-10% Pb+Pb碰撞中产生的γ-喷注事件，训练了一个流匹配生成模型，以估计来自喷注诱导流体响应的末态强子谱d^3N/dp_Tdηdφ。仅利用γ和喷注的初始空间和动量信息，该网络能够条件性地生成来自喷注诱导流体响应的边际末态强子谱，且与训练数据吻合良好。与完整的CoLBT-hydro模拟相比，该生成模型实现了约六个数量级的计算加速，同时忠实地保留了马赫锥状流体响应的前沿和扩散尾迹的统计特性及其对强子谱的贡献。

## Abstract
In high-energy heavy-ion collisions, propagation of the energy deposited into the medium by energetic partons that traverse the quark-gluon plasma (QGP) leads to Mach-cone-like jet-induced medium response. Full simulations of such jet-induced medium responses require a complete model such as the coupled Linear Boltzmann Transport and hydrodynamic (CoLBT-hydro) model that can carry out the concurrent evolution of both hard partons and the medium. Such full simulations on parallelized computers, however, are very resource-intensive and alternative simulation methods will be useful for more extensive physics investigations. In this study, we train a Flow Matching generative model with $γ$-jet events in 0-10$\%$ Pb+Pb collisions at $\sqrt{s_{\rm{NN}}}$ = 5.02 TeV from the CoLBT-hydro model to estimate the final-state hadron spectra $d^3N/dp_Tdηdφ$ from jet-induced hydro response. With only the initial spatial and momentum information of the $γ$ and jets, the network is shown to conditionally generate the marginal final-state hadron spectra from the jet-induced hydro response that agree well with the training data. This generative model achieves a computational acceleration of approximately six orders of magnitude compared to the full CoLBT-hydro simulations, while faithfully preserving the statistical properties of the front and diffusion wake of the Mach-cone-like hydro response and their contributions to the hadron spectra.
